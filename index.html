Motivation

Decentralization empowers people to apply their resources within their rights only, with out the involvement of a third party. If you take Blockstack, you are feeling free to use your own resources and you are the only owner who has the authority to access your data. However, there isn't a visual interface of what your data looks like and what dApps[1] are doing with it.

What is this?

Blockstack FE[2] is a Google Chrome extension which shows your data on Blockstack. This extension only works on pages that uses Blockstack's provided storage system. [3]
